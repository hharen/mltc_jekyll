What will you learn

  1. Strategies to help improve your website ranking

    2. Learn techniques to optimize websites for search engines, get discovered, and how to drive organic SEO traffic.

  2. On-page SEO

    3. All the data that search engines look for to help find you: Meta descriptions, alt tags, permalinks, creating and structuring the content, page titles, links.

  3. Off-page SEO

    4. Helping search engines to know that you’re trustworthy and popular. Backlinks, domain authority.

  4. Technical SEO

    5. All the details that can make the difference between your site & someone else's… speed, permalinks, xml sitemap, https, bread crumb navigation.

  5. How to set up Google Analytics

    6. Allowing you to track visitors and look after your SEO in the future.
